FitTrack – Meal Planning Application
📌 Overview

FitTrack is a web-based meal planning application designed to help users organize their daily and weekly meals efficiently. The platform allows users to create structured meal schedules, manage food choices, and maintain healthier eating habits. By planning meals in advance, users can maintain a balanced diet and avoid last-minute unhealthy food decisions.

The goal of FitTrack is to simplify meal organization and promote a healthy lifestyle through an easy-to-use digital platform.

🚀 Features

Create and manage daily meal plans

Organize weekly meal schedules

Add and edit meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner

Simple and user-friendly interface

Helps maintain structured diet planning

Easy meal management for healthier eating habits
